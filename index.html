    Who We Are

    UIE focuses on research projects that involve the application of neurotechnology, ethics, and animal behavior models. We work in collaborations with neuroscientists, psychologyst, economists, and engineers.

    Our lines of research

    Moral Psychology

    Explore moral issues with the help of experimental, digital and behavior-economical methods, helping to create the society we need.

    Psychophysiology

    Processing biosignals to explore our emotional responses and find useful behavior patterns.

    Psychopharmachology

    Exploring pharmachological substances in a practical approach, we strive to explore potential uses in animals.

    Cognitive Technology

    Combining science and software to create a simple, easy to use, technology to improve people lifes.
